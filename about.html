About RISE TODAY

🌍 English RISE TODAY is a space built for growth. It shares simple ideas, real-life lessons, and daily motivation — designed to help you move forward, even when progress feels small. This platform is not about perfection. It is about becoming better, one step at a time. Here, every word is meant to inspire action, shift mindset, and remind you that change is possible — starting today. --- 🌿 Afaan Oromo RISE TODAY bakka guddinaaf ijaarame dha. Yaada salphaa, barnoota jireenyaa fi kaka’umsa guyyaa guyyaan qooddachuun si fuulduratti tarkaanfachiisuuf hojjetama — yoo guddinni xiqqaa fakkaatus illee. Kun bakka guutuu ta’uu irratti hin xiyyeeffanne. Guddachuu irratti xiyyeeffata — tarkaanfii tokko tokkoon. Asitti, jechi hundi si kakaasuuf, yaada kee jijjiiruuf fi jijjiiramni akka danda’amu si yaadachiisuuf qophaa’e — har’a irraa jalqabee.
