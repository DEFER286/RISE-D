About RISE TODAY

🌍 English

RISE TODAY is a space built for growth.

It shares simple ideas, real-life lessons, and daily motivation — designed to help you move forward, even when progress feels small.

This platform is not about perfection. It is about becoming better, one step at a time.

Here, every word is meant to inspire action, shift mindset, and remind you that change is possible — starting today.

🌿 Afaan Oromoo

RISE TODAY bakka guddinaaf ijaarame dha.

Yaada salphaa, barnoota jireenyaa fi kaka’umsa guyyaa guyyaan qooddachuun fuulduratti Tarkaanfadhu— yoo Jalqabni xiqqaa fakkaatus irra kee Guddaa Ta'a.

Asitti Dirqama Guutuu Ta'uu Hin Barbaachisu. Guddachuu irratti xiyyeeffata — tarkaanfii tokko tokkoon.

Asitti, jechi hundi si kakaasuuf, yaada kee jijjiiruuf fi jijjiiramni akka danda’amu si yaadachiisuuf qophaa’e — har’a irraa jijjiirama jalqabi.
